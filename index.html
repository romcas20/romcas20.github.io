Equipment

I shoot with a Nikon 5200 with the standard 18-55mm lens. This DSLR is incredibly user friendly and great for advanced beginners. The body does not have a rotating motor for autofocus. However, you can use an AF-S lens, a lens that has its own motor, to use the autofocus mode.


Portraits

Portrait photography is all about capturing your subject in a way that defines who they are. Here, I like to use a shallow depth of field to keep the subject in focus while blurring the background.






Night Photography

Taking photos at night presents an obvious problem, there is little light available. To account for the low light conditions, a wider lens aperature should be used in combination with slower shutter speeds. Also, you can tinker with the camera's ISO to change its sensitivity to incoming light.
